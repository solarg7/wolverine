Welcome to Marvel Link!

The goal here at Marvel Link is to help you explore the vast universe that is Marvel comics. You've seen the movies and now you don't know where to start, well simply enter a movie into the search bar over on the left hand side (please limit searches to the new Marvel Cinematic Universe or the X-Men films). Once you've searched for the movie simply select the option you're looking for from the list of results. You then have the option to click on the movie poster that appears and find the comic that the movie is (loosely) based on, or you can select one of the characters that appear to find comics featuring that character.
